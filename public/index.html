What is ANGELS CLUB ?

ANGELS CLUB is a collection of 10,000 unique, randomly generated cyberpunks roaming on the Polygon blockchain as ERC-721 NFTs. They are unique digital collectibles and no two of them are exactly the same.

Owning a ANGELS CLUB gives you a membership card, and grants you access to members-only benefits!

THE STORY

Neon City, 21XX: The great REMIX Resurrection. After centuries of being oppressed by the big corporations and corrupt dictators, decades having to crawl in the shadows, here comes the new generation of CYBERPUNKS, a massive community of music and tech lovers ready to take over the city skylines, willing to give their lives to recover what was taken from them.
